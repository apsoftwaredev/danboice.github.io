Moments with the Stars

Posted on 2016-07-13   |   In Spiritual Moments   |     |  

I'm sitting by myself on a deck in the woods in Northern Arizona. A few moments ago I was reading about spirals on my phone after looking at some pictures possibly created by the debris of smashed atomic partials. It is about 20 to 25 minutes past midnight Wednesday, July 13th, 2016. I am far away from any nearby city with lights and the moon isn't out yet, so I can see stars very bright and clear twinkling slowly in the blackness of outer space above me. There are bats fluttering around in the night chasing bugs. The wind is blowing gently in the pine trees and on my skin. It is chilly and I shiver a little when a moist breeze blows lightly by my skin, giving me a small flush of goosebumps then fading away. Little insects are tapping the screen behind me and there is a steady rhythm of crickets chirping all around me out in the forest.

I almost died a few weeks ago and I am recovering from a major abdominal surgery. I came back from the dead and now I'm out here in the forest at night far away from everyone. I guess that could have been the end of the story for this one. Am I just a bag of molecules? If so, then it is strange that they do this sort of thing. Puzzling over things. And it stews, thinking about how clueless I am about this whole thing… Wow, that was fast. I look up and see a shooting star. All this just seems a little too fishy to be something a bunch of molecules would do. I can't imagine how it is possible. It's beautiful anyway whatever it is. I'm suddenly overwhelmed with gratitude and appreciation for it.

There is a soft immediate slowly building response in my body awareness field that feels breathtaking. It is a bit like the chills or goosebumps, but more delicate and internal, as if something were in my head with me quietly observing and listening. It is that gentle tingly feeling in my body awareness. Maybe that's what they are referring to when they talk about call Qi or perhaps a "Holy Ghost." I'm not sure. I have felt it before. It does appear every so often when I'm moved or ponder about certain things in times like these. It seems to like and respond to different thoughts and observations. It is very pleasant. Maybe just my unconscious mind playing tricks with me or something.. Or something else. I really don't know, but it is nice. I'll keep an open mind about it.

I take out my camera to see if it can capture the stars, but the camera only records blackness when I point it at the sky. I'll just sit here a while with it. I think I'll wish to share it with someone, who knows. A lot of people do this I think from time to time. It is therapeutic. And I'll try to remember to call it up in my mind whenever I need to remember it or go someplace like this when I realize I've forgotten something.
